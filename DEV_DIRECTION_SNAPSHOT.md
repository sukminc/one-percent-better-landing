# DEV Direction Snapshot

Date: 2026-03-15

## 1. Primary purpose

- `onepercentbetter.dev` is a hiring and trust site.

## 2. Hero direction

- Balance career credibility with philosophy / execution energy.
- Do not let the hero read like only a resume.
- Do not let the hero drift into vague or overly poetic language.

## 3. Poker positioning on dev

- Keep poker visible as a separate vertical section.
- Keep poker clearly secondary on the dev site.
- Poker should read as proof of seriousness, not the main hiring story.

## 4. Funding decision

- Funding is removed from the dev site.

## 5. Projects / featured direction

- Projects should feel like proof, execution, and build quality.
- Reduce the "coming soon showcase" feel.
- Featured work should support trust faster than hype.

## 6. About direction

- About should be more credibility- and career-centered.
- Reduce transition-period or exploratory framing.
- Chris should read as proven, active, and current.

## 7. Copy / tone direction

- Shorter.
- Sharper.
- Less explanatory.
- More direct.
- Prefer trust over hype.
- Prefer proof over abstraction.

## 8. Open decisions

- Whether the hero should lean even more explicitly toward recruiting.
- Whether product naming on the landing page should be simplified further for recruiter-fast reading.
- Whether the poker section should eventually link to a live separate surface.

## 9. Recommended commit title

- `refine dev landing for hiring-first trust surface`
